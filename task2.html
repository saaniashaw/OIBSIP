Priyanka Chopra

Miss World 2000, an Indian Actress and Producer


Priyanka Chopra


Highlights of her life:


  • Early life -Priyanka Chopra was born on 18 July 1982 in Jamshedpur, Bihar (present-day Jharkhand), to Ashok and Madhu Chopra, both physicians in the Indian Army.Her father was a Punjabi Hindu from Ambala.Her mother is a Bihari-Magahi Hindu from Jharkhand and is the eldest daughter of Dr. Manohar Kishan Akhouri, a former Congress veteran, and Madhu Jyotsna Akhouri, a former member of Bihar Legislative Assembly.

  • Modelling -Priyanka Chopra's admittance to the Miss India competition in 2000 proved to be the gateway to her celebrity. She was named first runner-up and went on to participate in that year's Miss World contest, where she was crowned the winner.

  • Acting career -She is known for choosing versatile roles in Hindi movies like Aitraaz, Burfi, 7 Khoon Maaf, Fashion, Bajirao Mastani, Mary Kom, etc. Priyanka portrayed her acting talent with a wide range of characters in these films for which she received great acclamation in various award functions.
